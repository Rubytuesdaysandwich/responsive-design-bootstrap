Contrast

Contrast is about creating visual interest by pairing together elements with dissimilar properties. It's how you catch the users attention. If every element is the same size, shape, and color, then nothing stands out and your design will look boring and unprofessional.

Alignment

Alignment helps organize and order the content, controlling how the eye flows from one component to the next. It is the organization and creation of invisible lines. Without alignment, users will have a hard time recognizing how elements relate to eachother, and your page will look messy and disorganized.

Repetition

Repetition is using the same or similar elements throughout the design. Using the same set of colors, font, and layout on your pages ties your website together as a whole unit.

Proximity

Proximity is the distribution of space between elements. Related items should be grouped together so that they will be viewed as a group, rather than as several unrelated elements.
